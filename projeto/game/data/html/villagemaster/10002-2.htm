Rebirth Manager

 A rebirth can be performed to obtain a book of rebirth.  A character can perform 3 rebirths.
 A book of rebirth can be used to create a Rebirth Cloak  (with extraordinary skills).
 A rebirth is a new birth, again you will become a novice  player (lv %lv%), without any skills. You will keep your items.
